# Iran War Update — 8 April 2026

**Ceasefire holds; talks to begin in Islamabad**

## Key Developments

- **US-Iran two-week ceasefire agreed** — Iran accepted the truce after agreeing to reopen the Strait of Hormuz. Trump suspended wide-scale attacks and called it "a big day for world peace."
- **Negotiations start Friday** — Direct US-Iran talks are set to begin in Islamabad on April 10.
- **Israel supports ceasefire, but Lebanon excluded** — Netanyahu confirmed Israel backs the truce but stated it does not extend to Hezbollah in Lebanon.
- **Oil prices plunge** — Markets reacted positively to the ceasefire, with oil prices falling sharply.
- **Attacks initially continued** — Reports indicate some strikes continued briefly after the ceasefire was announced before fully holding.
