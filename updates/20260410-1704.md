# Iran War Update — 10 April 2026 (17:04 UTC)

## 🕊️ Ceasefire Status: Fragile 2-Week Agreement in Effect

**US and Iran agreed to a two-week ceasefire on April 8**, brokered by Pakistan. The deal came hours before Trump's threatened deadline for a massive military bombardment on Iran.

---

## 📌 Key Developments

### Ceasefire Terms
- **Duration:** 2 weeks (April 8–22)
- **Key demand from US:** Complete, immediate, and safe reopening of the **Strait of Hormuz**
- **Iran's response:** "Possible" — subject to coordination with armed forces and "technical limitations" — a caveat critics say gives Iran room to define compliance on its own terms

### 🚨 Ceasefire Already Being Tested
- **Kuwait blames Iran** for drone strikes on its territory
- **Trump casts doubt** on whether the ceasefire is holding
- **Strait of Hormuz** status remains ambiguous — the key flashpoint

### 🗺️ Next Steps
- **VP JD Vance** heads to Pakistan on April 10–11 to lead peace talks
- Negotiations with Iran set to begin in **Islamabad, Pakistan**
- Goal: a **permanent deal** beyond the 2-week window

## 💰 Markets & Economy

- Analysts warn the Hormuz ambiguity could **derail the ceasefire** and reignite oil spike
